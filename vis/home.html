Political Advertisements in the 2015-2016 Election Cycle

How is political advertising different in different states? How is it different at different points in the election cycle? With a dataset from Professor Michael Franz of the Bowdoin College Government and Legal Studies Department, we seek to answer these questions and many more.

Navigate to the Project Description and the Data Description for background information, or head straight to the Data Visualization to explore the 2015-2016 election cycle in a totally new way!

A Project by Tom Lucy and Duncan Gans, Fall 2017
