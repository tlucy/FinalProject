Colophon

Creators

This website, data visualizations, and datasets were created by Tom Lucy and Duncan Gans in the Fall of 2017.

Collaborator

This project was designed and created with cooperation and input from Professor Michael Franz of the Bowdoin College Government and Legal Studies Department. Professor Franz supplied the dataset, and his research is focused on political campaign ads.

Style

  • Font: Georgia
  • Colors: #00a6ef, #e91d0e, #c76706, #d3d3d3

Languages

  • Python: used for data processing
  • HTML: used for web structure
  • CSS: use for page style
  • JavaScript: used for visualization creation and interactivity, the d3 library was heavily utilized
